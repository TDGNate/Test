About Us

What is POP Umai?

POP Umai is a start-up business established in late 2021. We sell numerous anime-themed desserts and aim to open up a brick and mortar location in the future. Our mission is to recreate the fun dining experience of a Japanese anime cafe for the anime community in Southern California.
